Natural, Intent-Based Control for Golf Mobility

The Most Natural Way to Control
Golf Course Mobility

POINGO is a patent-pending control system developed by California Suncap LLC to make golf mobility feel natural and predictable on real courses.

By understanding terrain — including slopes, elevation changes, and real-world course geometry — POINGO enables effortless, intuitive control for smart follow push carts, and significantly safer, more predictable behavior for low-speed golf carts on hills and uneven ground.

What We’re Building

POINGO is building a unified terrain-aware control platform that delivers natural, intent-based control across different types of golf mobility.

  • Smart Follow Push Cart — effortless walking with intuitive, minimal control.
  • Terrain-Aware Golf Cart — predictable behavior and enhanced safety on slopes and uneven ground.

The same core system interprets user intent from minimal inputs and orientation, then translates that intent into smooth, constrained, and context-aware vehicle behavior.

  • Minimal, learnable handheld interaction
  • Natural pointing and orientation-based control
  • Terrain-aware speed and behavior constraints
  • Designed specifically for golf-course environments
